Lasagna Recipe

Welcome to the lasagna recipe page!

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian comfort food that layers rich, savory sauce, tender pasta, and gooey melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this dish is sure to satisfy your hunger and warm your soul

Ingridiant

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the chopped onion over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the beef and onion mixture. Season with salt, pepper, and half of the fresh basil. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese until smooth. Stir in the remaining basil and parsley.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, a layer of sauce, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers two more times.
  8. Top the final layer with any remaining sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
