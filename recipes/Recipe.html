Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Description

This creamy Chicken Alfredo is a luxurious pasta dish thatâ€™s rich, indulgent, and perfect for dinner. Tender chicken breast is served over fettuccine noodles, smothered in a velvety Alfredo sauce.

The dish is easy to prepare, and with a combination of heavy cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese, it creates a silky sauce that pairs perfectly with the chicken and pasta.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Remove from the pan and slice.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly. Let the cream simmer for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Continue to cook until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat. Top with sliced chicken and fresh parsley. Serve immediately.
