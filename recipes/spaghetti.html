  • Spaghetti Recipe

    Description

    This spaghetti recipe is a timeless classic. With rich tomato sauce, perfectly cooked pasta, and savory meatballs, it’s the perfect dish for a family dinner or weeknight meal.

    To prepare this spaghetti, you’ll need ingredients like ground beef, tomatoes, garlic, and herbs. You’ll also make a simple meatball mixture, which will cook right in the sauce. This dish is both hearty and comforting!

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb spaghetti pasta
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 tablespoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1 egg
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Steps

    1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper. Form into small meatballs.
    3. In a large pan, sauté chopped onion and garlic until softened. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, and salt. Bring to a simmer.
    4. Add meatballs to the sauce and let them cook for 20-25 minutes until fully cooked through.
    5. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Top with extra Parmesan cheese, if desired.
